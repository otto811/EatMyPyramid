📅 Recall a Week's Tracking History

Pick a date (or use the dropdown) from any past week. The Day selector, Daily Log, Macros, Food Groups, and WW Points will switch to show that week's saved data for browsing.

Tip: While viewing a past week you can browse every day but logging, editing, exercise points, and resets are disabled (to protect your history). Click the ← Return to Live button (appears when active) or re-select the current week to resume normal tracking.
